About Me

Hello! I am currently a senior attending Northeastern Illinois University. I am majoring in Environmental Studies and minoring in Geographic Information Science, while already recieving my GIS certificate during the Fall 2022 semester. I have a passion for remote sensing, geospatial analysis and its applications while using GIS software.

QGIS

Using QGIS, I have compiled data from various sources such as the Chicago Data Portal and the U.S Census Bureau to create a map showing median household income and total population count within each Cook County census tracts using various spatial analysts tools within ArcGIS Pro.

Supervised Classification using ERDAS Imagine

For one of my final class projects, I decided to create a supervised classification map of Mexico City to determine how much urban expansion has occured from 2014 to 2019. Urban expansion is represented as red while green represents vegitation.

Extracting 3D Buildings from LiDAR Data

Using LiDAR data gathered for Turbog Havn located in Copenhagen, Denmark, I was able to extract 3D buildings from LiDAR points. These models can prove to be useful for future projects in the area such as urban expansion or other remote sensing projects.

FInding Suitable Areas for Housing Projects

Gathering data from various sources such as the Chicago Data Portal and the United States Geological Survey, I created a map to determine suitable areas in Cook County in regards to factors such as total population count and poverty rates within each selected census tracts. I used various spatial, geostatistical and 3D analysis tools to work with vector and raster data.

ArcPy, "R" Scripting and other skills

Using ArcPy, I am able to create scripts to automate processes which are capable of creating geodatabases, running different tools together, etc. Using the "R" programming language, I am also able to create scripts for statistical scenarios such as finding correlation between datasets. My other skills include:

-Proficient in Spatial Analysis, Geostatistical and 3D tools within ArcGIS Pro, QGIS.
